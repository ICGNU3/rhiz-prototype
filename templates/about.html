Why Rhiz Exists

The story behind building relationship intelligence for builders who activate meaningful connections

The Problem We Saw

In late 2024, we noticed something troubling. The people building the future were drowning in shallow connections while missing the deep relationships that could transform their work.

Founders were collecting LinkedIn connections like trading cards. Investors were playing relationship theater. Community builders were optimizing for engagement metrics instead of genuine trust. Everyone was networking, yet nobody was building lasting relationships with intentionality.

"We realized that in a world obsessed with scale, the most valuable resource (trust) was getting increasingly scarce."

What We Discovered

After talking to hundreds of builders, we found a pattern. The most successful people weren't the ones with the biggest networks. They were the ones who understood relationship context at a deeper level.

They knew not just who they knew, but how those relationships intersected with their goals. They tracked trust signals, reciprocity patterns, and relationship health. They approached networking like engineers approach systems: with intention, measurement, and continuous improvement.

They were doing it all manually, in spreadsheets and notes apps, because no tool existed for this level of relationship intelligence.

Our Insight

What if we could build relationship intelligence that matches the sophistication of the people using it? Not another CRM that treats contacts like database entries, but a system that understands the nuance, context, and intentionality behind meaningful connections.

We realized that AI could help surface patterns in relationship data that humans miss. It could identify when someone needs attention, suggest the right moment for an introduction, or recognize trust signals that indicate a relationship is strengthening or deteriorating.

"Rhiz exists because the future belongs to those who navigate relationships with the same intentionality they bring to building products."

Our Principles

Purpose-First

Every connection should serve a deeper purpose. We start with your goals and find the relationships that can help you achieve them.

Trust-Based

Real relationships are built on trust, not transactions. We help you track and nurture trust signals over time.

Intelligence-Driven

AI should enhance human intuition, not replace it. We surface insights that help you make better relationship decisions.

Community-Focused

The best networks are communities of builders who support each other's growth and success.
