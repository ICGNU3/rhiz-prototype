Trust Insights & Relationship Intelligence

June 14, 2025
v0.9.6

This week we tackled the core relationship intelligence features that make Rhiz unique.

🧠 AI Features

NEW Trust tier system (Rooted, Growing, Dormant, Frayed)
NEW Reciprocity analysis and scoring
NEW AI-powered relationship health assessments
NEW Contextual action suggestions

🎨 UI Components

  • Trust dashboard with real-time metrics
  • Interactive trust tier visualization
  • Relationship health scoring interface
  • Timeline of trust score changes

🔬 The Science

We spent a lot of time thinking about how to quantify trust without being creepy. Our approach focuses on interaction patterns rather than content analysis. Things like response times, reciprocity, and consistency matter more than what people actually say.

🎯 User Feedback

Early users love the trust insights but want more control over the scoring algorithm. We're planning to add customizable weightings for different trust signals.
